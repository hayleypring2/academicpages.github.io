Pepper Culpepper is Blavatnik Professor of Government and Public Policy at the Blavatnik School of Government and a Professorial Fellow at Nuffield College at the University of Oxford.

He is the Vice Dean for Academic Affairs and Blavatnik Chair in Government and Public Policy. His research focuses on the intersection between capitalism and democracy, both in politics and in public policy.
